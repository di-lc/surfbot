Famous for its long sandy stretch and open ocean, Barra da Tijuca holds many surf spots along the shore, with steady waves, wild nature and city vibes, drawing both beginners and seasoned riders chasing the perfect wave.

The area blends ocean and city life, with wide beaches, native greens and the perfect setup for surfing, giving sea lovers a unique place to connect with nature and the beach lifestyle.
